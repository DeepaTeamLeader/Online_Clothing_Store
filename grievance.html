Our commitment is to resolve your concerns promptly and efficiently.

Step 1: Initial Contact (Customer Support)

Most issues can be resolved quickly by our dedicated Customer Support team.

  • Contact Method: Live Chat, Email, or Phone Call.
  • Details Required: Order ID, Description of the issue, and desired resolution.
  • Resolution Time: We aim to acknowledge your grievance within 24 hours and resolve it within 3 business days.

Step 2: Escalation (Nodal Officer)

If you are unsatisfied with the resolution provided in Step 1, you may escalate your issue to our Nodal Officer.

Nodal Grievance Officer: [Officer Name]

Email: grievance@onlineclothingstore.com

Process: Please include the ticket number from your Step 1 complaint.

Response Time: The Nodal Officer will respond within 5 business days.

Step 3: Final Review (Appellate Authority)

If the Nodal Officer's resolution is still unsatisfactory, you may request a final review by the Appellate Authority.

This is the highest level of internal review. The decision at this stage will be considered final and binding from the company's side.

We are dedicated to fairness. Thank you for your patience as we work to resolve your issue.
