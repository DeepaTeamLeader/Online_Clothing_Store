❌ Order Cancellation Policy

We aim to process and ship your orders as quickly as possible. Please review our cancellation guidelines below.

⏱️

Time Window for Cancellation

You may cancel your order only if it has not yet been processed by our warehouse. This window is typically 2 hours after the order is placed, but may be shorter during peak sales periods.

✅

How to Submit a Request

  • If the "Cancel Order" button is visible in your My Orders dashboard, click it immediately.
  • If the button is unavailable, please contact our Customer Support team via email or chat with your Order ID.
⚠️

What Happens if it's Too Late?

If the order has been shipped, we are unable to stop the delivery. In this case, you must wait for the item to arrive and then initiate a standard return (see our Return Policy for details).
