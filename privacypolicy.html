Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This policy outlines what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use our online clothing store.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to process your orders and improve your shopping experience.

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, shipping address, billing address, phone number.
  • Payment Data: Encrypted payment details (processed securely by third-party processors).
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our site (via Cookies).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for legitimate business purposes:

  1. Processing and fulfilling your orders.
  2. Communicating with you about your order status or promotional offers (with consent).
  3. Improving our website functionality and marketing efforts.
  4. Preventing fraudulent transactions.

3. Data Security & Storage

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data transmission uses SSL encryption.
  • Access Control: Access to your personal data is restricted to necessary personnel.

4. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of marketing communications at any time.
